The mental model theory is a theory that tries to explain the reasoning mechanisms of the human being. This website provides a basic implementation of a modal logical variation of the theory.

Please use the following syntax for your expressions and separate your premises with a " , ". "(" and ")" should be used to separate logical operations. We support non-dyadic operations, so "(A ^ B ^ C)" is an Xor with three arguments, while "(A ^ B) ^ C" first evaluates the first Xor with A and B takes the result and connects it with the third argument C :

Operator Necessary Possibly Negation Or Xor And Implies Biconditional
Syntax [] <> ~ | ^ & -> <->

Both Necessary and Possibly are not yet fully implemented and are therefore not included in the webapp as of yet.
